#### 1. [重要] 智能体Token统计系统 (Agent Token)
- **新增模块**: `com.rrsjk.light.service.agent` 包
- **7类AI智能体**: PROPERTY_CERT_RECOGNITION(房产证), PV_TILT_RECOGNITION(组件倾角), INVERTER_RECOGNITION(逆变器), CONTRACT_RECOGNITION(合同), BANK_CARD_RECOGNITION(银行卡), LEASE_RECOGNITION(租赁), CONTRACT_DATE_RECOGNITION(合同日期)

- **数据来源**: `agent_token_view` 表 (已在 sql-table-refs 索引)
- **来源**: rrsjk-light-service (commits: 59213056→0c7ec4c8, baoxin+mabin, 2026-06-25~27) + rrsjk-merchant-web (AgentTokenController, commit fc3a6108)
